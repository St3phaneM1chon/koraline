# CLHIA Guideline G1 - Product Disclosure

**Source:** https://www.clhia.ca/en-CA/Industry/clhia-guidelines/g01-product-disclosure
**Scraped:** 2026-03-24
**Organization:** Canadian Life and Health Insurance Association (CLHIA)
**Date:** March 18, 2005
**Category:** Distribution, Product Disclosure

---

## Purpose

The purpose of this Guideline is to set out practices for printed disclosure (i.e. hard copy or electronic) by life and health insurance companies about their products, and thereby promote the ability of consumers to make informed decisions about the products for which they apply.

## Scope

This guideline covers all printed disclosure materials provided by life and health insurance companies regarding their insurance products. It aims to ensure that consumers receive sufficient, clear, and accurate information to make informed purchasing decisions.

## Key Principles

1. **Consumer-focused disclosure**: All product information should be presented in a manner that helps consumers understand what they are purchasing.

2. **Clarity and accuracy**: Disclosure materials should be clear, accurate, and not misleading.

3. **Complete information**: Key features, benefits, limitations, and exclusions of insurance products should be clearly communicated.

4. **Standardized practices**: Member companies are expected to adopt consistent disclosure practices across the industry.
