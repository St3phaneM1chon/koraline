## Overview

The Financial Services Regulatory Authority of Ontario (FSRA) ensures individuals and businesses meet the qualifications for a licence, are suitable to be licensed, and comply with all ongoing licensing requirements.

## Key Requirements (Life and A&S Agents)

1. **Education**: Complete the Life Licence Qualification Program (LLQP) course
2. **Exam**: Pass the LLQP exam (one year to apply from first pass date)
3. **Sponsorship**: Must be sponsored by a licensed insurer for first two years
4. **E&O Insurance**: Active errors and omissions policy ($1M minimum per occurrence)
5. **Criminal Background Check**: Through FSRA-approved vendor (Triton Canada, $19.15 fee, valid 90 days)
6. **Ontario mailing address** (no postal boxes)
7. **Personal email address**
8. **Suitability**: Good character and reputation, familiarity with Ontario insurance laws

## Key Requirements (General Insurance / OTL Agents)

1. **Exam**: Pass the OTL Agent's Exam (or hold CIP designation or equivalent RIBO exam)
2. **Sponsorship**: Must be sponsored by a licensed insurer
3. **Criminal Background Check**
4. **Ontario mailing address**
5. **Suitability**

## Fee Structure
- New life and A&S licence application: $170 (covers first 2 years)
- OTL application: $150
- Criminal background check: $19.15

## Continuing Education (Life and A&S)
- 30 CE credits required every 2 years for renewal
- Must be related to technical aspects of life insurance
- Qualifies: Legal/regulatory matters, fundamentals, financial planning, taxation, risk management
- Does NOT qualify: Sales techniques, marketing, recruiting, office skills, company-specific products

## Labour Mobility
FSRA has implemented new labour mobility licensing processes for interprovincial transfers.
