# FSRA Ontario - Life and Accident & Sickness Insurance Licence Application

**Source:** https://www.fsrao.ca/licensing/life-and-accident-sickness-agent/apply-new-life-and-accident-sickness-insurance-licence
**Scraped:** 2026-03-24
**Regulator:** Financial Services Regulatory Authority of Ontario (FSRA)

---

## About the Licence

### Sponsorship Required
- New agents must be sponsored by a licensed insurer for first two years
- Sponsoring insurer confirms all licensing requirements and manages application process
- Agent responsible for maintaining licence (CE credits, E&O insurance)
- After two years, moves to non-sponsored licence

### Fee
$170 for a new application (covers first two years). May be paid by sponsoring insurer.

## Requirements for a New Licence

### Education Program (LLQP)
1. **Complete the LLQP course** - Various approved course providers available
2. **Pass the LLQP exam** - Must apply for licence within 1 year of first passing the exam

### Errors and Omissions (E&O) Insurance
- Active E&O policy required in same legal name as licence
- Minimum $1 million limit for single occurrence
- Must include extended coverage for loss resulting in fraudulent acts
- Required for entire duration of holding licence (even if not actively selling)
- FSRA conducts random audits for compliance
- New agents may be covered by sponsoring insurer's E&O policy

### Checklist for New Licence
- Sponsoring insurer for first two years
- LLQP course and exam completed (first pass within 1 year)
- Appropriate E&O insurance
- Ontario mailing address (can receive registered mail)
- Personal email address
- Suitability for licence

## Application Process

1. Sponsoring insurer sets up application through Licensing Link
2. Insurer sends applicant two links:
   - **Link 1**: Criminal background check via Triton Canada ($19.15 fee, valid 90 days)
   - **Link 2**: Complete licensing application
3. Applicant verifies suitability:
   - Familiar with Ontario insurance laws
   - Will carry out business in good faith
   - Not engaged in business that would jeopardize integrity/independence/competence
   - Good character and reputation
   - Disclosure of bankruptcies, lawsuits, criminal records, regulatory decisions
   - LLQP exam score
4. Pay fee via Licensing Link (credit/debit card)
5. Submit application to FSRA

### After Submission
- FSRA sends confirmation email
- Most applications: Check processing times online, monitor Licensing Link
- Applications requiring additional review: FSRA contacts via email with assigned Specialist

## Post-Licensing Requirements

### Continuing Education (CE)
- 30 CE credits required over two years for renewal
- Keep certificates of completion for at least 4 years

### Qualifying CE Topics
- Legal, legislative and regulatory matters
- Fundamentals of life insurance / accident & sickness insurance
- Financial planning, taxation
- Client needs analysis
- Proper use of life insurance products
- Rating, underwriting and claims
- Accounting and actuarial considerations
- Risk management principles
- Policy contract provisions

### Non-Qualifying Topics
- Sales techniques, motivation, marketing
- Health/stress management
- Recruiting, office skills
- Management training
- Company-specific product training
